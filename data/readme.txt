About Dataset
Daily updates of the below Dow Jones indices, as well as S&P500, from FRED. Starting from 18-11-2013.

Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJIA)
Dow Jones Transportation Average (DJTA)
Dow Jones Composite Average (DCIA)
Dow Jones Utility Average (DUIA)
Standard & Poor 500 (SP500)
